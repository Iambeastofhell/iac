About IAC

The Industrial Academia Conclave (IAC) at IIT Palakkad is a flagship event that bridges the gap between academic research and industrial application. As a vibrant platform where innovation meets expertise, our mission is to bridge the gap between industry and academia, fostering meaningful connections and progress.

At IAC, we believe in the power of collaboration to shape the future. Our events, including panel discussions, lecture series and talks by Industry leaders, and exhibitions, are designed to inspire, educate, and connect. From exploring cutting-edge technologies to addressing real-world challenges, our programs facilitate a rich exchange of ideas and perspectives.

IAC is where potential is realized, innovation knows no limits, and connections ignite fresh ideas. This is more than just a platform—it's a catalyst for change, where collective expertise drives impactful outcomes. Whether you're an industry professional, academic, or innovator, IAC offers the ideal environment to connect, learn, and grow.

KeyNote Speakers

Dr. Sreedevi Upadhyayula

Dr. Sreedevi Upadhyayula is a seasoned chemical engineer with a rich blend of industry and academic experience. After earning her M.Tech in Chemical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur in 1993, she worked as a Senior Design Engineer in both the public and private sectors. In 1998, she returned to academia as a CSIR Senior Research Fellow at the National Chemical Laboratory (NCL), Pune, and completed her PhD in 2001.

She then pursued postdoctoral research at the University of Notre Dame, USA, before starting her academic career as an Assistant Professor at IIT Kharagpur in 2004. Currently, she is the CLASS of 66 Chair Professor at IIT Delhi. Her research focuses on sustainable heterogeneous catalysis, zeotype materials in petrochemical and refinery catalytic processes, biomass conversion to biofuels, catalyst design, parametric study, kinetics and modeling of heterogeneous reactions.

Dr. Satya Chakravarthy

Dr. Satya Chakravarthy is a Professor of Aerospace Engineering at IIT Madras, where he has been a faculty member since 1997. He earned his B.Tech from IIT Madras and M.S. and Ph.D. from Georgia Tech, USA. His research focuses on propulsion and combustion in gas turbines, rocket engines, and electric aircraft. He leads the National Centre for Combustion Research & Development (NCCRD) at IIT Madras, the largest center of its kind globally, and the DRDO Centre of Propulsion Technology. He has received several prestigious awards, including the HAL Prize, the Young Engineer Award by the Indian National Academy of Engineering, and the DRDO Academic Excellence Award.

Beyond academia, Dr. Chakravarthy is the Founder and Technical Lead of The ePlane Company, which is developing hybrid aerial vehicles. He is also a director at Agnikul Cosmos and Aerostrovilos, focusing on space and power generation technologies, respectively. Additionally, he mentors Team Avishkar Hyperloop at IIT Madras and advises several tech startups, including NewTrace and GalaxEye. Dr. Chakravarthy is an accomplished speaker with TEDx talks and numerous online lectures, sharing his expertise in combustion and aerospace engineering with a global audience.
